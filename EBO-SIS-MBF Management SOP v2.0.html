📋 POD Audit Process

🎯 Proof of Delivery (POD) Audit Implementation

Enhancing control over LSP partners through comprehensive POD verification and accountability measures

🔍 What is POD Audit?

  • All received PODs from LSP Partners undergo rechecking and cross-verification by Bestseller Outward team
  • Each POD is reviewed to verify store stamps and operational remarks
  • Clean POD: No remarks noted, indicating no wear/tear or discrepancies
  • Clear POD: Clean PODs reviewed by Operations Team with no additional remarks
  • Comprehensive review ensures missing remarks are captured before final approval

🎯 Why POD Audit is Required?

  • Enhance control over LSP partners through clear, documented processes
  • Streamline operations while ensuring accountability and transparency
  • Improve stock acceptance accuracy with clear POD remarks and stamps
  • Establish comprehensive tracking and quality control measures

👥 POD Audit Responsibility Matrix

POD Audit - Team Responsibilities & Expectations

🏢 SCM Team Responsibilities

  • Conduct thorough review of all POD documents
  • Focus on identifying remarks and stamps on PODs
  • Categorize each POD: Clean POD, Clear POD, or POD with Remark
  • Cross-verify with LSP Partner submissions
  • Generate audit reports every 10 days

🏪 Operations/Store Team Responsibilities

  • Conduct thorough stock acceptance checks
  • Provide clear remarks and stamps on PODs during stock acceptance
  • Review all Clean PODs within 7-day period
  • Communicate discrepancies to DC Outward & Discrepancy Team
  • Maintain accuracy and quality standards

👔 Area/City Manager Responsibilities

  • Provide clear justification for late discrepancy submissions (after 7 days)
  • Approve case closures for delayed discrepancies
  • Ensure team compliance with POD audit timelines
  • Oversee audit process effectiveness

📋 Key Guidelines

🕒 Time-Critical Actions
• GRN completion within 48 hours
• Continuous PO follow-up for pending orders
• FIFO basis dispatch for Non-PO orders
📧 Communication Protocol
• All dispatches require email communication
• Discrepancy reporting via discrepancy.sis@bestseller.com
• MIS circulation to all stakeholders every EoD
🔍 Quality Control
• Track short/torn/soiled/damaged boxes
• Maintain proper MIS documentation
• Follow STRICT discrepancy reporting protocol
